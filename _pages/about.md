Sainan ZHANG is a Postdoctoral Associate at George Washington University. She received her PhD. degree from Dalian University of Technology, China, and a Bachelor's degree from Henan University. Previously she worked as a Research Associate in the Department of Applied Mathematics of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) in April 2024 and as a Research Assistant at the Chinese University of Hong Kong from March 2022 to March 2024. Her research interests include Stochastic Programming, Distributionally Robust Optimization, Preference Robust Optimization, and Chance-Constrained Stochastic Programs.
